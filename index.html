About Us

Savco, a subsidiary of Aboullaban Group, is at the forefront of the Egyptian market as a distinguished leader in The Refining, Packing & Distribution of Cooking Oil & Ghee.

Leveraging cutting-edge technologies in oil refining, the company is committed to delivering superior quality products that exceed the expectations of our customers.

Established in 1997 in Syria, Savco relocated its headquarters to Egypt in 2013.

Marking a pivotal moment in its journey towards expansion and growth.

Since then, it has steadfastly built upon its legacy of excellence, focusing on innovation and continuous improvement to maintain its position as a trusted provider of high-quality cooking oils and ghee in Egypt.

Savco has a Vision to be The Preferred Provider of High-Quality Cooking Oils & Ghee in Egypt and the wider region, recognized for its standards, innovation, and customer-centric approach. It aims to consistently deliver products of exceptional quality that meet the diverse culinary needs of its customers.

Savco also operates as A Distributer for Sister Companies that provides wide range of FMCG products.

Quality Assurance

MANUFACTURING & QUALITY ASSURANCE

At Savco, the team prioritizes quality in every aspect of its operations, from sourcing raw materials to the final stages of distribution.

Savco operates state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities in Egypt, equipped with advanced technologies to ensure the highest standards of production and quality assurance. Its facilities are strategically organized to support efficient operations across various phases of production

MANUFACTURING FACILITIES

QUALITY CONTROL

dedicated areas equipped with modern testing equipment for rigorous quality control of raw materials and finished products.

RAW MATERIAL WAREHOUSES

Secure storage facilities for incoming raw materials, ensuring optimal inventory management.

REFINING PHASES

Specialized units for the refining processes tailored to different types of cooking oils.

FILLING & PACKING PHASES
